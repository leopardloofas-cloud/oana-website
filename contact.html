The Artist

Oana
Ruxanda

I am an emerging photographer, born in 1989, based in Bucharest, working mostly with analogue processes. A flâneur at heart, I often carry my camera with me, drawn to moments that frequently pass unnoticed and sometimes hold a quiet story I have lived myself.

My practice moves between observations of everyday life — including incidental, sometimes humorous encounters with people — and contemplative studies of remote landscapes. I am interested in how individuals and environments shape one another, as well as in transience and material memory. Natural light, colour, texture, and weather play a central role in shaping the atmosphere of my images.

In recent works, I have expanded my practice into mixed media, incorporating materials collected from the places I photograph and blurring the boundary between image and physical presence.

This website is an evolving space. New works will be added gradually, stay tuned.
