Between Cities

2024
Year
2024
Category

Images
12 photographs
Location

Between Cities is a meditation on movement and stillness — capturing the moments that exist between departure and arrival, between who we were and who we are becoming.

Shot over six months across Eastern European cities, this series explores the quiet poetry of public spaces: waiting rooms, empty platforms, figures caught in transit. Each frame asks the same question: where does a person truly belong?
